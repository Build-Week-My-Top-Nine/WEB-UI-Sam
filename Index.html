What is MyTopNine?

MyTopNine is a new app that allows users to sort their own unique list of their favorite things!

From a list of pre-made awesome things, to users inputting their own, users will be able to show off their interests to new people!

Our Purpose

Ever move to a new city? Maybe transferred to a new school? Forgotten how to get out and make new friends? MyTopNine is here to help with that!

By allowing people to create their own prioritized list of things they love, they can see others who also share interests. Meeting people with a common interest helps get through those awkward "icebreakers" and straight to new adventures!

How Does It Work?

It's as easy as 1,2,3,4...you get the point! In just a few simple steps, you can create a new account, select a user portrait, and start ranking your top Nine things!

But, what if nothing that interests me is available? We've got you covered! There's a fantastic way to submit your OWN special interests! What's better than that? Glad you asked! OTHER users will be able to both view and select the entry you placed! Now you and your future friend can share your love of underwater basket weaving!
